Shigeru Miyamoto went on to create Link, Samus, and the modern iteration of
Donkey Kong. But Mario remained his most personal creation — a character born
of constraint, shaped by accident, and perfected through iteration.

In interviews, Miyamoto has said that he never imagined Mario would outlast a
single game. "I thought we would make one game," he said, "and that would be it."
He was wrong about that. More gloriously wrong than perhaps anyone in history.
