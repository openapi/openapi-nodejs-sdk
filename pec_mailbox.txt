The pixel constraints led to a series of brilliant design decisions. Giving the
character hair would have required animating it, so Miyamoto gave him a cap instead.
A mouth was too small to animate expressively, so a thick mustache was added to
define the face without needing lips.

The red overalls were chosen to make arm movements visible against the body,
and the blue shirt contrasted clearly with the red. Every element of Mario's
iconic appearance was born from a technical limitation, not artistic preference.
