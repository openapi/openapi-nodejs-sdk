Mario's origins are a story about limitations becoming strengths. A failed product
line became an iconic character. Technical pixel constraints became a timeless design.
A landlord's name became a global legend. An overdue rent bill named a cultural icon.

Every element of Mario — his cap, his mustache, his profession, his world — was born
from necessity and transformed by imagination. He is, in every sense, a character
conceived by accident and perfected by genius.

The full story is hidden across the history of this repository,
one fragment per commit — if you know where to look.
