Miyamoto has described Mario's design as a symbol. The character was intentionally
generic in ethnicity and profession so that players anywhere in the world could
project themselves onto him. The red hat, the mustache, and the blue overalls
became one of the most recognized silhouettes on the planet.

Not through accident — but through deliberate design choices made under
technical constraint. The limitations did not diminish the character.
They defined him.
