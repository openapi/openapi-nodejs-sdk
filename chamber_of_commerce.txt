Donkey Kong became a massive success. In North America alone, it grossed over
100 million dollars in its first year. The game's three-phase structure — the
girder level, the cement factory, and the elevator level — gave players a sense
of narrative progression.

Miyamoto had created something the arcade world had never seen: a game with a
beginning, a middle, and an end. An experience, not just a challenge.
